About Project

Purpose: developing an application that allows to monitor class attendance both from the teacher and from the parent side and also provide automated calculation for generating monthly payments.

Our application will help simplify interaction in the teacher-parent chain. The teacher will be able to keep an electronic log of participations, as well as automatically generate an invoice based on recorded absences. The parent, in turn, will be able to control the attendance of a particular lesson, as well as receive a ready-made invoice, which will save him from the need for unnecessary calculations.
